GeminiBlu.com Review: Is GeminiBlu Limited a Legit Forex Broker?

GeminiBlu.com review reveals a suspicious and likely deceptive setup masquerading as a professional Forex and CFDs broker. While the website uses sleek design and industry jargon to project credibility, deeper analysis raises several red flags.

Domain History and Timeline

The domain geminiblu.com was initially registered in 2008. According to ICANN Lookup, it was updated in May 2025. Historical data from Internet Archive shows it was previously used for selling e-learning materials until it went inactive in 2016. The domain was then repurposed in 2025 by a new entity now posing as a brokerage platform.

This practice is common among shady financial projects that want to appear older and more established than they really are, fooling potential clients into assuming a long track record of service.

Who Owns GeminiBlu?

The company behind the project appears to be GeminiBlu Limited, registered in Manchester, UK in April 2025. This aligns perfectly with the timeframe when the broker site began operations. According to Endole.co.uk, the company is newly incorporated and has no financial or operational history in the financial markets.

Is GeminiBlu Licensed?

One of the major red flags in this GeminiBlu review is the broker’s claim of being licensed by the FCA, FinCEN, and MSB. However, a check on the FCA Register reveals no listing for GeminiBlu Limited. FinCEN and MSB registrations are not licenses for forex brokerage operations either.

The so-called MSB Registration Number: 31000293629205 displayed in the website’s footer is vague and unverifiable. No physical address or meaningful regulatory proof is provided.

Deceptive Marketing and Fake Claims

GeminiBlu’s website is bloated with marketing buzzwords such as “cutting-edge features” and “universal accessibility,” which add no real value. They claim to serve over 500,000 traders and boast an average payout of $18.7 million monthly—yet the website itself has only been live for a few weeks.

Such blatant misrepresentation is a major concern and should not be ignored by prospective investors. Their statistics appear entirely fabricated to lure in unsuspecting users.

So, Is geminiblu.com a Scam?

In light of the above, it’s fair to question, Is Gemini Blu legit? All evidence points to a high likelihood that this is a scam operation. From unverified licensing, misleading claims, and an opaque ownership structure, GeminiBlu Limited does not meet the standards of a trustworthy financial service provider.

Investors should exercise extreme caution and avoid depositing any funds with this broker.
